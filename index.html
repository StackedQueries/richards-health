FAQ

Good fit, clear expectations

A few common questions, answered directly. If you’re still unsure after reading, you’re welcome to ask during your strategy review.

Do you offer employer group health plans?

No. This advisory specializes exclusively in individual and self‑employed coverage structures. That focus allows for deeper work on plan design, exposure, and long‑term fit for owners and independent professionals.

Is this only marketplace coverage?

No. Marketplace and private options are evaluated depending on what’s available and suitable in your state. The goal is to choose the structure that best fits your risk profile and business—not a single channel.

Do you charge advisory fees?

No. I don’t charge advisory or consultation fees. I’m paid only when you enroll in a plan I recommend—so my compensation is tied to you choosing coverage, not to the review itself.

How long does the review take?

Initial structured reviews typically take 20–30 minutes. You’ll leave with a clearer understanding of your current exposure, potential alternatives, and next steps if you choose to move forward.
